Zzip is a mid-end outerwear fashion brand. Their goal is to provide outerwear that is casual yet luxurious, contemporary, and comfortable.

Zzip targeted audience are people who are looking for clothing that are well designed and well made at an affordable price. They cater mainly towards customers who are young adults and adults, ranging from 16 - 35 year olds.
