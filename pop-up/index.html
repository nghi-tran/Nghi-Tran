LET'S MAKE PIZZA BRAID

This animation is created for PSAid in order to promote their campaign of "Cash is Best." The idea behind the campaign is that cash travels easierr, more effectively, and cause less waste than other types of donation. This animation was created to bring a bright and exciting feel to the audience so that the message catch their attention easier.

This video campaign was placed second in the competition hosted by PSAid.
